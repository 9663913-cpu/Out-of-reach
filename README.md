# Out of Reach
Out of Reach is a 3d first person obstacle course game with stretchy arm mechanics about a super human escaping a government lab.
UML![tickle](https://github.com/9663913-cpu/Out-of-reach/blob/main/images/Out%20of%20reach.drawio.png)
## core loop 
* Break out of your room
* manuever through the halls
* swing over booby traps
* go through vents
* escape through the front door
